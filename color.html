Kindergarten Teacher

The reason I decided to chose this type of color scheme for a kindergarten teacher is because it seems fitting for a site aimed at a young audience. Because the site is meant for young children and they have short attention spans, their eyes will be drawn to the bright colors used in the palette.

Bank

The reason I decided to use this dark/neutral color scheme is because it is for a bank, which may want to be viewed more professionally by others (business men, companies, sponsors, adults).
