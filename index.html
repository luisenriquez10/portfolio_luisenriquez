Web Design Unit Reflections

    • What do you think are the most important vocabulary terms (or key concepts) in this unit? What do you think that they mean?

    • What do you think are the most important skills that you learned in this unit of study?

      • I think that some of the most important skills in this unit of study is the ability to create lists and the ability to properly size my headers to show the difference in importance of one title or header to another.

      • I've gotten better at knowing some of the HTML basics and basic knowledge over the Html language.
